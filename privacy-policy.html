Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

At DeleteThisFile, we take your privacy and data security extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our secure file deletion service.

1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

DeleteThisFile operates on a zero-knowledge architecture. This means:

  • Files you drop for deletion are processed entirely on your local device.
  • No file content or metadata is ever transmitted to our servers.
  • Your files never leave your computer during the deletion process.
  • We maintain no logs of files deleted or content processed.

2. Information We Collect

DeleteThisFile collects minimal anonymous usage data:

  • Technical Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous aggregated metrics like total deletions performed and average processing time.
  • Performance Data: Non-identifiable technical data to help us optimize application performance.

We explicitly do not collect:

  • File names, types, or contents
  • File metadata or timestamps
  • User identifiable information
  • IP addresses or precise geolocation

3. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous data we collect is used for:

  • Improving the performance and user experience of our application
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Calculating aggregate statistics for our global deletion counter
  • Researching patterns to enhance security protocols

4. Enterprise Customers

For enterprise deployments, additional data processing terms may apply:

  • Enterprise customers may opt into enhanced usage analytics for their organization's deployments.
  • Enterprise admins may access aggregated deletion metrics for compliance reporting.
  • Custom data processing agreements are available for enterprise customers.
  • All enterprise data handling complies with GDPR, CCPA, and relevant data protection regulations.

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-leading security practices to protect our service and your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure development lifecycle with code reviews
  • Compliance with international security standards

6. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access any personal data we hold about you (note: we collect virtually none)
  • Right to erasure of any stored information
  • Right to object to processing of your data
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@deletethisfile.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the revised policy on this page with an updated revision date.
